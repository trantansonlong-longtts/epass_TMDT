## 3. GOI Y TAO MUC LUC TU DONG TRONG WORD

Sau khi ban dan noi dung vao Word, hay lam theo cac buoc sau de tao muc luc tu dong:

1. Boi den tung tieu de chinh nhu `1. THONG TIN CHUNG VE DE TAI`, `2. LY DO CHON DE TAI`...
2. Gan style `Heading 1` cho cac muc lon.
3. Gan style `Heading 2` cho cac muc con nhu `6.1`, `6.2`...
4. Dat con tro vao trang muc luc.
5. Vao `References` -> `Table of Contents` -> chon mau muc luc tu dong.
6. Khi sua noi dung xong, bam `Update Table` de cap nhat so trang.

## 4. GOI Y DINH DANG CHUAN DE NOP BAO CAO

- Font chu: Times New Roman.
- Co chu: 13 hoac 14.
- Gian dong: 1.3 hoac 1.5.
- Le trang:
  - Tren: 2 cm
  - Duoi: 2 cm
  - Trai: 3 cm
  - Phai: 2 cm
- Tieu de chuong: in dam, viet hoa, can trai hoac can giua tuy yeu cau.
- Danh so trang: bat dau tu phan noi dung chinh.

## 5. GOI Y BO CUC FILE WORD HOAN CHINH

Thu tu sap xep trong file Word nen la:

1. Trang bia
2. Loi cam on
3. Loi mo dau
4. Muc luc
5. Noi dung bao cao chinh
6. Tai lieu tham khao
7. Phu luc hinh anh

Bo cuc nay se giup bai bao cao trong day du, chuyen nghiep va dung tinh chat mot bai nop hoc phan.
